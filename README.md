# the-love-schema

In linguistics, there is a syntactical rule that adjectives have to appear in a particular word order. 

That order is the following:

1. quantity or number
2. quality or opinion
3. size
4. age
5. shape
6. color
7. nationality or place of origin
8. material
9. type
10. purpose

This database (composed of 15 - 20+ adjectives per individual), similarly describes the personal characteristics (e.g. physical features, personality, etc.) of two fictional characters in an unrequited love relationship in order to demonstrate their dating compatiability. It includes 15 - 21+ adjectives per individual and represents their personas as potential clients in a dating compatibility case study for a dating service.
